Hack the Fog 2.0

On March 3rd, we ran San Francisco's first high school hackathon. With people from the San Francisco and Oakland area, we saw various hacks ranging from simple websites to mobile applications and VR. We were also featured in the San Francisco Chronicle:

We organized Hack the Fog 1.0 because we wanted an event that was highly accessible to high school students in the San Francisco and Oakland area as well as many of our peers. Over the course of 24 hours from March 3rd to March 4th, we saw that happen.

For Hack the Fog 2.0, San Francisco's second high school hackathon, we're looking to increase the number of attendees as well as the impact we have.
